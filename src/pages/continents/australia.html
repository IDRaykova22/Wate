About Global
warming in Australia

Changes

  • Increased temperatures contribute to more frequent and severe heatwaves. Changing precipitation patterns and prolonged droughts impact water resources.

Impacts

  • Risks to agriculture, water scarcity, and threats to biodiversity, especially in sensitive ecosystems like the Great Barrier Reef.

How can we help?

As an individual

  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk instead of relying on personal vehicles. Embrace a circular economy by reducing, reusing, and recycling.

As a community

  • Engage in community gardens, promote local and sustainable food production, and support renewable energy initiatives. Advocate for stronger environmental regulations.
