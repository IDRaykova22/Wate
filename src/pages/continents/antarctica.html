About Global
warming in Antarctica

Changes

  • Warming temperatures contribute to the melting of Antarctic ice shelves and glaciers, leading to rising sea levels globally.

Impacts

  • Accelerated ice loss contributes to sea-level rise, impacting coastal regions worldwide. Changes in ecosystems affect wildlife, particularly penguins and seals.

How can we help?

As an individual

  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk instead of relying on personal vehicles. Embrace a circular economy by reducing, reusing, and recycling.

As a community

  • Engage in community gardens, promote local and sustainable food production, and support renewable energy initiatives. Advocate for stronger environmental regulations.
