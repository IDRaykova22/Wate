About Global
warming in Africa

Changes

  • Increased temperatures and changing rainfall patterns affect agriculture, water availability, and food security. Desertification poses a threat to many regions.

Impacts

  • Vulnerability to droughts and heatwaves, affecting both rural and urban populations. Shifts in disease patterns may also occur.

How can we help?

As an individual

  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk instead of relying on personal vehicles. Embrace a circular economy by reducing, reusing, and recycling.

As a community

  • Engage in community gardens, promote local and sustainable food production, and support renewable energy initiatives. Advocate for stronger environmental regulations.
