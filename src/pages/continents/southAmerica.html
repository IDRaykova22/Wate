About Global
warming in South America

Changes

  • The Amazon rainforest, a vital carbon sink, is at risk due to higher temperatures and changing rainfall patterns. Glacial retreat in the Andes affects water supply for downstream communities.

Impacts

  • Increased frequency of extreme weather events, such as floods and droughts, poses risks to agriculture and ecosystems.

How can we help?

As an individual

  • Use public transportation, cycle, or walk instead of relying on personal vehicles. Embrace a circular economy by reducing, reusing, and recycling.

As a community

  • Engage in community gardens, promote local and sustainable food production, and support renewable energy initiatives. Advocate for stronger environmental regulations.
